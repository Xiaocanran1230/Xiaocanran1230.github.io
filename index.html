About Me

Hello! I am Canran Xiao, a postdoctoral fellow at Sun Yat-sen University. My postdoctoral mentor is Prof. Li Shen. I received my Ph.D. from Central South University, advised by Prof. Xiaohong Chen and Prof. Yongmei Liu.

My research focuses on data management, data intelligence, and data economy. I study how data can be organized, governed, valued, and transformed into trustworthy data-driven intelligence, decisions, and value, with an emphasis on connecting robust data infrastructure, data-driven intelligence, and market-oriented data value creation. I have published over twenty papers in leading venues and journals including NeurIPS, AAAI, ICLR, WWW, CVPR, ACM MM, IEEE Transactions, and Neural Networks.

I am always interested in collaborations that connect data management, data intelligence, and data economy, especially around data quality, data value, and trustworthy data-driven learning.
