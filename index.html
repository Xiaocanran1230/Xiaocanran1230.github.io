About Me

Hello! I am Canran Xiao, a postdoctoral fellow at Sun Yat-sen University. My postdoctoral mentor is Prof. Li Shen. I received my Ph.D. from Central South University, advised by Prof. Xiaohong Chen and Prof. Yongmei Liu.

My research is centered on data intelligence. I study how data can be learned from, attributed, valued, and transformed into trustworthy intelligent systems. My work focuses on dynamic data learning, heterogeneous data learning, and data attribution and valuation, spanning continual and personalized learning, multimodal and federated settings, and value-driven data selection, augmentation, and synthesis. I have published over twenty papers in leading venues and journals including NeurIPS, AAAI, ICLR, ICML, WWW, CVPR, ACM MM, IEEE Transactions, and Neural Networks.

↻ Dynamic Data Learning

Learning from continuously evolving data for adaptive, continual, and personalized intelligent systems.

 ⧉ Heterogeneous Data Learning

Building robust intelligence over multimodal, cross-domain, federated, and non-IID data.

 ∑ Data Attribution and Valuation

Quantifying data contributions to guide data selection, augmentation, synthesis, governance, and value creation.

I am always interested in collaborations on data intelligence, especially data problems for foundation models, evolving and heterogeneous data, federated and unlearning settings, data attribution, data valuation, data augmentation, and data synthesis.
